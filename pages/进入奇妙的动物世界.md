<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: KaiTi;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;text-align:justify;">进入奇妙的动物世界</h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;text-align:justify;"> 万态保育集团致力于通过探索奇妙的动物世界促进母语学习。 今年，集团邀请学校和家庭利用取景框（viewfinders）探索动物展区和线上教学资源，让孩子、教师、家长挑战制作可在户外或室内使用的环保取景框。取景框可与文字游戏和开放式问答配合使用，让孩子在了解动物世界、学习语言时更具互动性。此外，发挥创意制作取景框的过程也能提供一个真实的学习情景，让孩子思考如何通过循环利用一般废弃物品，达到保护野生动物和它们的栖息地的目的。</p>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">Mandai Wildlife Group (MWG) would like to share how explorations of the wonderful world of animals can enhance the learning of Mother Tongue Languages. Schools and families are invited to explore the animal exhibits and online resources using viewfinders. Our viewfinders use word games and open-ended questions to combine language learning and learning about the animal kingdom through interactive and meaningful activities. MWG also provides a challenge for all viewers to create eco-friendly viewfinders. The creative process of making viewfinders out of discarded objects presents an authentic opportunity to talk about how we can protect wildlife and their habitats by reusing items we would otherwise just throw away.</p>

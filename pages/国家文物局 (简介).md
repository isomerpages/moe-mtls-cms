<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: KaiTi;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;text-align:justify;">新加坡国家文物局</h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;text-align:justify;">      欢迎大家与国家文物局一起通过文化遗产认识母语。<br/><br>
在此，讲华语运动、新加坡马来语文理事会及淡米尔语言理事会将分享丰富的资源，鼓励公众一起学习母语。<br/><br>
公众也有机会深入了解新加坡的文物机构，包括印族文化馆、马来文化馆及晚晴园—孙中山南洋纪念馆。<br><br/>
 在这里，父母可以找到适合孩子学习母语的活动，教育工作者也可以探索课堂以外的学习机会。</p>
 <h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: Lato,sans-serif;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;">National Heritage Board</h4>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">Everyone is welcome to discover the heritage and culture of our Mother Tongue Languages with the National Heritage Board (NHB).<br/><br>
Find out more about language programmes, tips and resources from the Speak Mandarin Campaign, Malay Language Council, Singapore and Tamil Language Council.<br/><br>
Learn more about the Heritage Institutions including the Indian Heritage Centre, Malay Heritage Centre and Sun Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall.<br/><br>
Parents can find out more about the learning opportunities available for the young, while educators can explore opportunities to expand learning beyond the classroom. </p>

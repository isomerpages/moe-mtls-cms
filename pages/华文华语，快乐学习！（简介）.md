<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: KaiTi;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;text-align:justify;">华文华语，快乐学习！</h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;text-align:justify;"> 新加坡华文教研中心致力于推动华文教与学。根据新加坡华文教学的实际需要，中心不仅出版相关的教学配套、学术论著与学生读物，也开发教学资源、电子平台与应用程式。此外，中心每年都会主办各种比赛，其中， “创意无限”全国儿童故事创作大赛与“我们是小网红” 全国小学生短视频制作比赛，目的是为了激发学生对华文华语的兴趣。 “2022新加坡幼儿华语文教具比赛” 则希望鼓励新加坡幼儿教育工作者发挥创意，将寓教于乐的巧思融入教学。</p>
<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: Lato,sans-serif;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;">Chinese, Joyful learning!</h4>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">The Singapore Centre for Chinese Language (SCCL) is committed to promoting the teaching and learning of Chinese Language. SCCL is dedicated to research studies that are closely associated with the teaching and learning of Chinese language.  It strives to provide Chinese Language teachers and students with resources and applications that cater to their teaching and learning needs. Competitions such as the “2022 National Vlog Competition for Singapore Primary School Students”, “Creativity, No Boundaries National Children’s Story Writing Competition” and “2022 National Preschool Teaching Aid Competition” promote and engage students’ interest in the learning of Chinese.</p>

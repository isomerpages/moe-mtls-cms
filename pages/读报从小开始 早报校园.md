<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: KaiTi;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;text-align:justify;">读报从小开始 </h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;text-align:justify;">  新报业媒体为不同年龄层的学生推出具本地特色、内容新颖有趣的学生报，其中包括适合学前孩童阅读的《小小拇指》、适合小学低年级学生阅读的《小拇指》、适合小学高年级学生阅读的《大拇指》和适合中学生阅读的《逗号》。</p>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;text-align:justify;">    此外，早报校园也不断完善网络学习平台zbschools.sg。学生可借助多元的网络学习工具，自行观看视频、阅读新闻，培养自主阅读的习惯，让华文学习更加多元、有趣。</p>
<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: Lato,sans-serif;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;">Reading The News From A Tender Age</h4>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">SPH Media Limited has launched four student publications with educational values and fun elements that cater to pre-school, primary and secondary school students. These are Thumbs Up Little Junior, Thumbs Up Junior (lower primary), Thumbs Up (upper primary), and zbCOMMA.</p>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">The student publications have also launched an enhanced online learning portal, zbschools.sg. This platform enables students to explore engaging content and videos using self-learning tools. Students can enjoy a fun and interactive process for learning Chinese while cultivating the habit of reading from a young age.</p>

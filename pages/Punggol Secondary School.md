<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: KaiTi;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;">就地循环模式的混合式学习</h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;">    培道中学母语部采用Michael B Horn & Heather Staker的“就地循环模式”作为进行混合式学习的教学方法。此模式让学生轮换到不同学习站进行小组协作、个人学习，并让教师给予学生反馈。通过利用科技工具，这种模式还可以与居家学习、在线学习和面对面学习相结合。此教学设计有助于提高学生的学习参与度，并促进课堂上的协作式学习。
</p>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;">Punggol Secondary School’s Mother Tongue Department adopts the “Station Rotation Model” from Michael B Horn & Heather Staker, as a blended learning approach. This involves small group collaboration, individual learning and teacher feedback, each of which takes place as students rotate from one station to another. Leveraging on ICT tools, this approach also allows a combination of home-based learning, online learning and face-to-face learning. This pedagogical model is suitable for second language learners as it promotes the joy of learning through student collaboration.</p>

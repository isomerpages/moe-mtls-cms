<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: Lato,sans-serif;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #a3c864;">Lihat-Fikir-Tutur </h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;"> ‘Lihat-Fikir-Tutur’ merupakan satu projek yang diusahakan oleh Unit Bahasa Melayu dan Bahasa Tamil di Sekolah Rendah Waterway. Projek ini memberikan fokus kepada pengajaran lisan bergambar secara interaktif bagi murid Darjah 3 dan Darjah 4. Melalui proses Pembelajaran Teradun (Blended Learning), Rutin Berfikir (Thinking Routines) dan Pengajaran Pembezaan (Differentiated Instruction), murid berpeluang untuk mempertingkat kemahiran bahasa, terutamanya kemahiran interaksi lisan. Murid digalakkan untuk menjana idea secara kolaboratif melalui penggunaan platform ICT seperti SLS dan Padlet. Dengan itu, murid dapat menyusun pemikiran mereka dan bertutur dengan lebih yakin tentang gambar serta memberikan pandangan dan berkongsi pengalaman mereka.      </p>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">Project ‘See-Think-Speak’ is a combined effort by the Malay Language and Tamil Language teams of Waterway Primary School. It focuses on honing the picture conversation skills of the Primary 3 and 4 students. Students develop their language and interaction skills through blended learning, thinking routines and differentiated instruction strategies. They are encouraged to make their thinking visible through collaborative learning, leveraging on ICT platforms such as SLS and Padlet. 
Through this project, students will be able to speak and describe a picture confidently by providing their opinions and sharing their personal experiences. </p>

<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: KaiTi;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;">探究乐园</h4>
<p style="font-size: 20px;font-family: KaiTi;text-align:justify;">  德行小学设计的“探究乐园”活动，旨在丰富中、高年段学生的语文学习体验。四个年级的校本活动分别是“小书迷”、“小记者”、“小文学家”和“小探索家”。活动采用了探究式学习方式，通过各层次的引导问题和思维策略，拓展学生的思维，激发他们的学习兴趣。学生还可以通过线上平台进行协作学习，展示自己的学习成果。在“探究乐园”中，学生快乐地学习语文，积极地积累和运用所学，最终收获丰硕的成果。
</p>
<h4 style="font-size: 35px;font-family: Lato,sans-serif;padding-top:12px;margin:10px;color: #d84178;line-height: 1.5">Inquiry-based Learning (IBL) Experience in Corporation Primary School</h4>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">Corporation Primary School aims to enrich Primary 3 to Primary 6 students’ knowledge and learning experiences of the Chinese language through an Inquiry-based Learning (IBL) programme. This structured school-based programme features the following series of activities: “Young Book Lover”, “Young Journalist”, “Young Writer” and “Young Explorer”.  </p>
<p  style="font-size: 20px;font-family:Lato,sans-serif;text-align:justify;">Students’ interests are slowly aroused as they learn through questioning and critical thinking strategies, and work in teams to display their work on online platforms. The programme enables students to experience joy in their learning and helps reinforce Chinese language competencies through encouraging students’ active participation in their learning.</p>
